Chord Sequencer

Instructions

The Chord Sequencer is an online tool designed to help songwriters.
If you get stuck writing a song and you don't know what the next chord in your sequence should be OR
you just need to find some Modal inspiration.
Chord Sequencer is here to help!

1

Key

Use the up and down arrows to select a key. This will be the root note of the scale.

2

Mode

Select a mode from the buttons below. This will determine the notes in the scale.

3

Chords

Click on the chord buttons to hear the chords in the scale. Fiddle around until you find something you Like.

4

Note Length

Click on the note length button to change the length of the note when you press a button.

  • 1/1: Is a whole note - A really long note - use with caution - notes can overlap and annoy your cat.
  • 1/2: Is a half note - A little shorter
  • 1/4: Is a quarter note - The recommended length - not too short - not too Long
  • 1/16: Is a sixteenth note - really short and cheeky
